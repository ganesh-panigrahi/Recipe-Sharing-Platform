BARBIE MILKSHAKE

Course : Dessert, Snack
Cuisine : American
Keyword : Barbie, Barbie Milkshake, Barbie Recipes, Frozen Strawberries, milkshake

prep time

5 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

1 Shake

instructions

step 1

Make the Barbie Milkshake
In a blender, combine the milk and ice cream and blend for a few seconds.

step 2

Add the frozen strawberries or mixed berries and dragon fruit powder or substitute.

step 3

Blend until smooth and creamy.
Serve topped with whipped cream and sprinkles. Enjoy!


tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups

ingredients

1 cup vanilla ice cream

1 cup milk, I used 2 % dairy milk but, any milk will work. Remember though, the color might be slightly different for each shake depending on what kind of milk you use such as almond or oat milk.

1 tsp dragon fruit powder, more or less depending how much you want the dragon fruit depth and how you want the color to come out. 1 cup frozen Strawberries, for a thicker shake, add a few more strawberries, you can also throw in another berry such as blueberries or blackberries for a more purple color.

Whipped cream for garnish, aerosol, or homemade

Pink and/or purple sprinkles, nonpareils, candy hearts, anything goes for garnish. Save these for Valentine's Day
